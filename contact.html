Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I add my skills?

Visit the "My Skills" page and use the form to add your skills along with your proficiency level. You can add as many skills as you've learned, and update them anytime.

Is KYOMATOS free to use?

Yes! KYOMATOS is completely free for learners looking to gain experience and find opportunities that match their skills.

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our intelligent algorithm analyzes your skills, proficiency level, learning sources, and preferences to match you with opportunities that best fit your profile and career goals.

What types of opportunities are available?

We offer a wide range of opportunities including internships, freelance projects, volunteer positions, entry-level jobs, and contract work across various industries.

Can I edit my profile later?

Absolutely! You can update your skills, proficiency levels, and preferences anytime through your profile. Your matches will be updated accordingly.

How long does matching take?

Our algorithm works instantly once you've added your skills. You'll see matched opportunities immediately on your dashboard.
